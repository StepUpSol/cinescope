    1 · The Genre Tides

    Annual share of releases by genre. Streamgraph reveals when each genre crested and when it receded. Hover any band to read its year-by-year story.

    2 · The Profit Galaxy

    Production budget vs worldwide gross on a log–log canvas. The diagonal is break-even; everything above it earned money back. Drag a rectangle to lock the other charts onto that slice.

    3 · Critics vs Crowd

    Rotten Tomatoes (critics) against IMDb (audience). The four quadrants split films into Universal Hits, Critic Darlings, Crowd-Pleasers, and the Universally Panned. Click a quadrant to keep only its films.

    4 · Profitability Heatmap

    Median return on investment for every genre, every decade. Diverging color: orange means money lost on the median film, teal means money made. Cells are clickable.

    5 · Director Leaderboard

    Top 15 directors in the current view, ranked by aggregate worldwide gross. Bars decompose into individual films on hover. Click a director to filter every other chart to their work.

    6 · Runtime DNA

    Ridgeline of runtime distributions per genre. Where the ridge bulges is where the genre lives. Documentaries cluster short, epics drift long, comedies stay tight around 95 minutes.

    CineScope · Data: Vega Movies (3,201 films, cleaned to 2,913). Built with D3.js v7.
